Meatballs

Easy Italian style meatballs with simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Jimmy Dean's Regular sausage
  • 1/2 cup crushed saltine crackers
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano Cheese
  • 2 Garlic cloves minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon Parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar

Directions

  1. Lightly beat eggs with salt, pepper, sugar, garlic and parsley
  2. Add crushed crackers and grated cheese
  3. Add the meat in slowy until everything is evenly distributed and do not over mix
  4. Make 2 oz balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover with a foil
  5. Bake for 45 minutes at 335 degrees (flip over after 23 minutes)
