Chicken Teriyaki

Simple and authentic chicken teriaky recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken (breast or thigh)
  • Cooking oil
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon giner minced
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon Mirin
  • 2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1&1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon water

Directions

  1. Cut chicken inti bitesize pieces, or keep it the way it is.
  2. Rub the chicken with cornstarch
  3. For the sauce, mix mirin, sugar, soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger and water. Heat it up in the microwave for 1 minute.
  4. Add some oil to a pan and brown the chicken
  5. Add sauce to pan and put the lid on. Cook on low heat for 5 minutes
  6. Garnish with green onion
