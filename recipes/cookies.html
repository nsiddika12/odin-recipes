Chocolate chip cookies

Classis chocolate chip cookie recipe from Nestle Toolhouse

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter/shortening
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Prep a baking sheet with parchmetn paper, preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. With a hand-mixer, beat butter and sugar until creamy
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mix until creamy
  4. Add salt, baking soda, flour, mix with a spatula
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix with the spatula
  6. Make into 2 oz balls, bake for 12 minutes at 325 degrees
